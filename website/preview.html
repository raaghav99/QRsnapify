Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free? No hidden costs? +
Yes, completely free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscription. Ever. The app is built for personal use and that's not changing.
Which Android version does it support? +
Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. It's tested on Android 12 and 13 primarily.
Where is my scan history stored? +
Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no server. If you uninstall the app, your history is gone — there's no backup.
Why do I need a Gmail account for early access? +
Google Play's early access program requires a Gmail address to add you as a tester. It's a Play Store requirement, not ours.
How long does early access last? +
You'll need to keep the app installed for 14 days — that's how long Google requires testers to run the app before it can go live for everyone. After that, it stays on your phone normally.

Ready to snap?

QRSnapify is live on Google Play for early access users. Sign up and get access within 24 hours.
