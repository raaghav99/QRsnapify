Why do I need a Gmail to install? +
Google Play's closed testing requires us to manually add each tester's Gmail before they can install the app. There's no public link — every tester has to be approved one by one. That's the only reason we ask.
Why 14 days? +
Google requires at least 12 testers to keep the app installed for 14 continuous days before approving a public launch. No surveys, no tasks — just keep the app on your phone and use it normally.
Is the app free? +
Yes — completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no hidden payments. Forever.
Which Android version do I need? +
Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher. Works on most phones made after 2016.
