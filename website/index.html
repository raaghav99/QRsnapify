Why do we need your Gmail? +
Google Play's closed testing requires us to manually add each tester's Gmail before they can install the app. We can't just publish a public link — every tester has to be approved one by one.

That's the only reason we ask. We store it in our private tester sheet, don't share it with anyone, and won't use it for anything other than sending you the access link.
Why 24 hours? +
We add testers manually, not automatically. Once you sign up, we review the submission, add your Gmail to Play Console, and send you the access link. This usually happens within a day.
What happens after you install? +
Google requires at least 12 testers to run the app for 14 days before we can apply for a public launch. That's it — no surveys, no tasks, just keep the app installed and use it normally.
